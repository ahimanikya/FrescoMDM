Manage->Relationships->Maintain

View and maintain existing relationships depending on the user's current access control. This allows exploration of relationships by showing associated relationships.

As with all the other screens details about a record and relationship properties can be viewed by selecting a record.


Maintain - By Relationship

Use Cases 

  • View and maintain all the records in a relationship
  • View details of selected relationships to see details of source and target record
  • View relationship properties for selected relationship

Maintain - by Record

Use Cases

  • View and maintain relationships for a single record to all its associated records.
  • View details of selected record (either main record or record in a relationship)
  • View relationship properties if a relationship is selected - by selecting a record within that relationship.
