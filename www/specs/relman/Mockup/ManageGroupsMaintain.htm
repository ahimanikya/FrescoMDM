Manage->Groups Instance->Maintain

Displays information about records and how they are grouped. Either a user can find records associated with a group instance or find the group instances that a specific record is associated with.

Use Cases

  • Maintain group instance.

    For example, a group called Household was created.  This group has many household instances such as "333 33rd St Apt2", "Broadway and 10th", ... that represent the people who live at a specific address.   
  • View the group instances associated with a specific record.
  • View the records within a group instance.
  • View details about a specific records within a group instance.
  • View the properties (fixed and extended attributes) associated with a specific group instance. 

Records in a Group Instance


  • User wants to find records associated with a group instance
  • User may want to add records to the selected group instance
  • User may want to remove records from a selected group instance
