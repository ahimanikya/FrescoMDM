Administer->Hierarchy

Provides a template for hierarchies of records within a domain. The user can view and maintain these templates which are then used by the maintenance hierarchy screens.

Use Cases

  • View and maintain hierarchy templates including associated child and parent relationships.
  • View and maintain attributes that are associated with the hierarchical relationship, such as how long the relationship is good for.
  • View and maintain extended attributes for the specific hierarchy.
  • View and maintain associated relationships such as child and parent relationships. A hierarchy must define a child relationship but a parent relationship is optional.
