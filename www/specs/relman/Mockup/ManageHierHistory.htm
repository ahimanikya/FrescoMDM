Manage->Hierarchy->History

Shows the history of how the hierarchy changed.

Use Cases



  • View when changes where made to a hierarchy during a specific time frame. This includes when fields where added, deleted or moved in a hierarchy.
  • User wants to find when something was changed either for legal reasons or because a hierarchy does not look right to them.
