Manage->Categories->Maintain

Displays information about how records are categorized. A user can either find what records are assigned to a category or find the categories associated with a specific record.

Use Cases

  • Maintain categories of records. Categories are hierarchical but should look more like a menu system.
  • View the categories associated with a specific record.
  • View the records within a category.
  • View details about a specific record within a category.
  • View the properties (fixed and extended attributes) associated with a specific category record.
